- Open the **Create ACL Policy** page:

    1. Open the GUI for your Vault instance.

    1. Login under the namespace for the policy or select the namespace from the
       selector at the bottom of the left-hand menu and re-authenticate.

    1. Select **Policies** from the left-hand menu.

    1. Select **ACL Policies** from the left-hand menu.

    1. Click **Create ACL policy +** on the **ACL Policies** page.
