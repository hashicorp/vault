- Open the **Create Group** page:

    1. Open the GUI for your Vault instance.

    1. Login under the namespace for the group or select the namespace from the
       selector at the bottom of the left-hand menu and re-authenticate.

    1. Select **Access** from the left-hand menu.

    1. Select **Groups** from the left-hand menu.

    1. Click **Create group +** on the **Groups** page.
